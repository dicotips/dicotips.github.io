Rubik's Cube Solver With The Shortest Path
Masters Thesis for UCB - 2008

Rubik's Cube solver with a lego robot. The software resolves the cube with the shortest path using heuristic Bidirecional search. I use Cube structure symmetry to reduce the space of solutions by a factor of 1 to 18.
