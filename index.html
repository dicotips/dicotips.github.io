Biography



I was born in Santa Cruz, Bolivia on 1982. I went to a catholic elementary, middle and high school. I grew up playing soccer, basketball and video games almost every day. On 1994, my mom bought our first computer, and I got fascinated about it.

When I was senior in high school, I liked to use the computer so much that I decided to join to the Catholic University of Bolivia to become a Computer Scientist. After I graduated, I started working on a top tier bank in my country, Credit Bank BCP. I joined the Marketing division and worked along with business oriented professionals, which improved my perception about finances and investments.

After persuing two master degrees, in Bolivia and in Spain, I moved to Santiago, Chile on 2010 for a PhD probram in Computer Science with an scholarship from the Chilean Government. I learned a lot about applied data science in the program and I worked on multiple projects in Astronomy, Earthquake detection and Social Media Analysis.

On 2013, as part of a published paper on the ACM KDD conference, I visited Chicago, IL. It was my first time in the U.S. THen I got the oportunity to work at Google in Mountain View, CA on 2014 as part of my internship.

I moved permanently to Seattle, WA on late 2016 to start working at Amazon AWS.

Now I am starting my own business ..... and in the near future will tell you more about it :)
